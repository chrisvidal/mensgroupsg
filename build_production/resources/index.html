Men’s Group Singapore is a non profit, volunteer based organisation established for men in 2018. Open to any man of any age, we offer authentic communication and connection, minimising the isolation many men experience through open, regular and accessible men’s group meetings. There are no political or religious affiliations and new participants are very welcome.
